Who is Anna?

Musician

Anna is a Latin Grammy nominated musician, winner of 2010 International Trumpet Guild Solo Competition, and graduate of the Manhattan School of Music. You might have seen her recently performing on America's Got Talent with Alex Boye. Check out Anna's very outdated music site.

Entrepreneur

Anna is the founder and operator of Juice Crawl, the world's first pub crawl for juice. Juice Crawl seeks to provide a platform for and build a community of people who want to have fun in a healthy atmosphere. Juice Crawl has been recently featured on Seth Meyer's Late Night Show, the Dr. Oz Show, and Jimmy Kimmel Live as well as many national and local news outlets. Anna designed the website and then translated her design to code.

Programmer

Anna is a self-taught programmer. You can view her profile at various online sources: Khan Academy, codecademy, and codefights. She also enjoys learning from coursera, lynda.com, Stanford Engineering Everywhere and reading Eloquent Javascript. Below is a program inspired by codefight's problem of the day. Enter a number and the program will converts your number into binary code. It will also tell you if the binary code it returns is symmetrical or not.

Anna enjoys programing because it involves creativity and problem solving - two skills a musician is very familiar with. Anna would love to sharpen her programming skills at General Assembly in order to create beautiful things for the world and continue advocating healthy living through Juice Crawl.
